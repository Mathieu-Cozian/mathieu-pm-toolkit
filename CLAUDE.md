# Mayday PM Toolkit — Context

## What is Mayday

Mayday is a **Knowledge Empowerment** platform for customer service teams.
Positioning: **No KM, No AI** — Mayday is the trusted third party that makes AI reliable by structuring the knowledge layer beneath it. Not a ticketing tool, not a Drive, not an intranet.

## Product Suite

| Module | One-liner | Key metric |
|---|---|---|
| **Mayday Knowledge** | Internal knowledge base for agents | −20% search time |
| **Mayday Selfcare** | Customer-facing self-service portal | −40% incoming tickets |
| **Mayday Academy** | Integrated training platform with gamification | −48% onboarding time |
| **Mayday AI** | AI layer across all modules (Genius + Ask Mayday) | Copilot for agents & admins |

## Key Personas

- **Knowledge Manager (KM)** — admin who creates and maintains the knowledge base; works via **Mayday Admin**
- **Agent** — frontline customer service agent; works via **Mayday Desk** (or the browser widget)
- **Directeur·rice de la Relation Client (DRC)** — economic buyer; cares about ROI, CSAT, and operational efficiency
