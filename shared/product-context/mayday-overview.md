# Mayday — Product Overview

**Category** : Knowledge Empowerment — pure player in knowledge management for customer service. Not a ticketing tool, not a Drive, not an intranet.
**AI positioning** : No KM, No AI. Mayday is the trusted third party that makes AI reliable by structuring the knowledge layer beneath it.

## Suite Logic
The four modules are not independent products — they share a single knowledge base as the source of truth:
- **Knowledge** — authors and maintains the KB internally (agents + KMs)
- **Selfcare** — publishes a curated subset of the KB publicly (end customers)
- **Academy** — trains agents using KB content (embeddable directly in modules)
- **AI** — is fueled by the KB across all three surfaces (No KM, No AI)

A feature in one module often has cross-product implications. Content authored in Knowledge can simultaneously appear in Selfcare, be embedded in Academy modules, and be queried by AI agents.
